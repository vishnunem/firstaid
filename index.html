Importance of first aid

  • First aid is important because it relieves pain, preserves life, prevents illness or injury from getting worse, and promotes recovery.
  • It is essential in situations where the victim is in a critical position and fatalities could have occurred.
  • The aims of first aid are to preserve life, prevent harm, and promote recovery.
  • First aid also helps maintain the flow of oxygenated blood through cardiopulmonary resuscitation (CPR)

About first aid

  • First aid is the immediate care a sick or injured person gets.
